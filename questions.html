Questions

What time are the ceremony and reception?

Guests are asked to arrive and be seated by 8pm. The ceremony will begin at 8:15pm. The reception will follow the ceremony.

The celebration will continue until 11pm—Please join us whenever you can!

What should I wear?

Sunday best or dressier is welcome. The wedding is outdoors, so please dress comfortably!

Will there be food at the reception?

Yes! Refreshments will provided at the reception.

Do I need to stay for the whole reception?

Not at all! Feel free to come and go as you need—we're grateful you can join us, no matter how long you're able to stay!
