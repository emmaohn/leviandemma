Questions

What time will the ceremony and reception be at?

The Ceremony will begin at 8pm, and the reception will follow. If you plan on attending the ceremony, please plan to arrive a little early.

The reception will go until 11pm, so drop by when you can!

What should I wear?

Sunday dress and nicer is acceptable for attendee attire. It will be summer, so dress accordingly!

Will there be food at the reception?

Yes! There will be some light refreshments at the reception.

Do I need to stay for the whole reception?

Nope! You can come and go whenever you need. We are always happy that you could come celebrate with us, no matter how long!
